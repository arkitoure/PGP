## Summary

This proposal seeks approval to allocate a continuation grant of **10T USDC** to **Physis Labs** for **Ship P1**, the first phase of the May shipping cycle following PHYPRIL.

With PHYPRIL complete, the project moves from campaign activation into **Ship**, a focused execution period dedicated to polishing and releasing the ecosystem’s foundation products to the community and broader market. Ship P1 covers the next two weeks and supports final product refinement, onboarding readiness, release coordination, and targeted launch marketing where required.

---

## Impact

* **Why Now:**

  * PHYPRIL created the activation window; May is now focused on shipping tangible ecosystem products.
  * The next two weeks are critical for tightening product experience, release flow, and community delivery.

* **Opportunities:**

  * Finalize and release the first ecosystem foundation products.
  * Strengthen community confidence through visible delivery.
  * Expand onboarding capacity for SmartSpot and ASTRALIS.
  * Convert PHYPRIL awareness into real product usage and participation.

* **Challenges:**

  * Multiple products are moving toward release in parallel.
  * Product polish, documentation, onboarding, and marketing must remain synchronized.
  * Scope must stay focused on shipping rather than reopening core architecture.

* **Future Vision:**

  * Ship P1 begins the transition from activation-stage momentum into an ecosystem with usable products, active onboarding, and a stronger foundation for wider adoption.

## Explanation

* **Implementation Overview:**

  * DAO will allocate **10T USDC** to Physis Labs to support:

    * Final polish and release preparation for **SmartSpot One**
    * Final polish and release preparation for **PRIVÉ**
    * Final polish and release preparation for **APERTURE**
    * Continued development and packaging of **ARQEN**
    * Release coordination for **Franca + Orb**
    * ASTRALIS onboarding expansion and supporting infrastructure
    * Ship-phase marketing, content, and communication where required
    * Documentation, QA, release coordination, and community support

* **Practical Examples:**

  * Finalizing product pages, onboarding flows, and operator materials.
  * Preparing release assets and support channels for community access.
  * Coordinating SmartSpot One and SYNCRAL-related shipment and onboarding follow-through.
  * Strengthening APERTURE and PRIVÉ readiness for user-facing release.

* **Addressing Corner Cases:**

  * Any unused funds will be carried forward or returned to Treasury subject to Council review.
