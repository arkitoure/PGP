## Summary

This proposal seeks approval to allocate a continuation grant of **10T USDC** to **Physis Labs** for **Activation P7**, extending the Activation framework into the **PHYPRIL pre-stage**, with campaign activation scheduled for **April 17**.

Activation P7 continues SmartSpot rollout, operator onboarding, and system stabilization while advancing preparation for the PHYPRIL campaign. This phase deepens execution across existing objectives while aligning the ecosystem for coordinated visibility and engagement.

---

## Impact

* **Why Now:**

  * Activation P6 has expanded the operator base and stabilized launch conditions.
  * The PHYPRIL pre-stage requires coordinated preparation to ensure a strong activation mid-April.

* **Opportunities:**

  * Continue scaling SmartSpot deployments and onboarding throughput.
  * Prepare ecosystem messaging, materials, and engagement pathways for PHYPRIL.
  * Strengthen alignment between product readiness and public visibility.

* **Challenges:**

  * Managing concurrent activation and campaign preparation without introducing instability.

* **Future Vision:**

  * Activation P7 positions the ecosystem for a synchronized transition into PHYPRIL, combining live network activity with structured public engagement.

## Explanation

* **Implementation Overview:**

  * DAO will allocate **10T USDC** to Physis Labs to support:

    * Continued SmartSpot shipment coordination and operator onboarding
    * Stability monitoring and system iteration under active usage
    * Documentation refinement and onboarding clarity improvements
    * PHYPRIL preparation (content, coordination, rollout readiness)

* **Practical Examples:**

  * Increasing active node count through continued onboarding.
  * Preparing campaign assets and engagement flows ahead of mid-April.
  * Iterating onboarding experience based on live user behavior.

* **Addressing Corner Cases:**

  * Any unused funds will be carried forward or returned to Treasury subject to Council review.
