## Summary

This proposal seeks approval to allocate a continuation grant of **11T USDC** to **Physis Labs** for **Activation P5**, extending the Activation framework established in PGP60–PGP63.

Activation P5 continues the controlled transition from beta stabilization into active launch conditions. The focus during this phase remains on shipping hardware, Franca integration with new client tools (PCC) and API access, refining documentation, and ensuring network stability while  usage expands.

---

## Impact

* **Why Now:**

  * Activation P4 initiated the transition into launch conditions.
  * Activation P5 continues that momentum as hardware deployment and public onboarding increase.

* **Opportunities:**

  * Expand early operator network.
  * Maintain disciplined rollout while monitoring system reliability.
  * Strengthen operator experience and confidence during launch.

* **Challenges:**

  * Increased user activity requires careful monitoring and support.
  * Shipping logistics and onboarding support must scale smoothly.

* **Future Vision:**

  * Completion of Activation P5 positions Physis for sustained post-launch operations and broader ecosystem participation.

---

## Explanation

* **Implementation Overview:**

  * DAO will allocate **11T USDC** to Physis Labs to support:

    * Continued shipment coordination and logistics
    * Operator onboarding and support
    * Documentation refinement and deployment guidance
    * Stability monitoring and iterative improvements

* **Practical Examples:**

  * Processing new SmartSpot operator deployments.
  * Monitoring network health during active launch conditions.
  * Updating onboarding materials based on real user behavior.

* **Addressing Corner Cases:**

  * Any unused funds will be carried forward or returned to Treasury subject to Council review.
