## Summary

This proposal seeks approval to allocate a continuation grant of **12T USDC** to **Physis Labs** for **Activation P6**, extending the Activation framework established in PGP60–PGP64.

Activation P6 continues the live rollout of SmartSpot hardware, operator onboarding, and ecosystem stabilization. This phase also overlaps with the early preparations for **PHYPRIL**, the project's annual marketing and visibility window, which will begin to introduce broader awareness around the ecosystem.

---

## Impact

* **Why Now:**

  * Activation P5 expanded the early operator base and strengthened launch conditions.
  * Activation P6 sustains rollout momentum while the network continues to stabilize under expanding participation.

* **Opportunities:**

  * Continue growing the SmartSpot operator network.
  * Support increased onboarding activity and early ecosystem adoption.
  * Begin light promotional coordination in advance of PHYPRIL.

* **Challenges:**

  * Maintaining network reliability as participation grows.
  * Ensuring onboarding, documentation, and support scale effectively.

* **Future Vision:**

  * Completion of Activation P6 transitions the ecosystem toward a fully operational network with sustained operator activity and increasing public awareness.

---

## Explanation

* **Implementation Overview:**

  * DAO will allocate **12T USDC** to Physis Labs to support:

    * Continued shipment coordination and logistics
    * Operator onboarding and support
    * Documentation and deployment guidance improvements
    * Stability monitoring and system iteration
    * Initial preparation for the PHYPRIL marketing window

* **Practical Examples:**

  * Processing additional SmartSpot deployments.
  * Monitoring system health during ongoing launch activity.
  * Updating onboarding and support materials based on user feedback.

* **Addressing Corner Cases:**

  * Any unused funds will be carried forward or returned to Treasury subject to Council review.
